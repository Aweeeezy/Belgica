Belgica Rodriguez

B elgica Rodriguez earned a B.A in Theatre Arts with options in acting and dance in spring of 2013 from California State University East Bay. Belgica has had a very fun and rewarding 2017! She is currently an Instructional Support Assistants at the Theatre and Dance Office in CSUEB and is thrilled to be a part of the department and help students get many opportunities and connections to the Bay Area theatre scene! In February, Belgica collaborated with current CSUEB students, staff, and alumni to bring the 2017 Vagina Monologues to CSUEB which she directed. Belgica’s future is looking bright as, this spring, she signed with Marla Dell Talent Agency for their Fit Modeling, Commercial, Print and On Camera divisions! In the past nine months, Belgica has modeled and acted in web-commercials for BrainTree, Square, CreditKarma, SunRun Solar, Verizon, and more! In April, Belgica was seen on stage at ZSpace Below for a staged reading of “Sucia” by Nicole Jost. You can see Belgica on stage next at Piano Fight for their annual Short Lived Festival in “Please Seat Yourself” by Min Kahng for round five (July 13th-15th) and again for the finals (August 3-5th) where her team from round 2 will compete for $5,000! Follow Belgica on social media for more.
